# 📈 Evaluation Bar

The evaluation bar is a feature that displays the current evaluation of the position on the board. It is a great tool for streamers to provide insights into the game for their viewers.

![Hello](/img/eval-bar.png)

## How to Enable/Disable the Evaluation Bar

<Callout emoji="⚠️">
  **Important**: Changing the evaluation bar setting requires you to restart your stream.
</Callout>

To enable or disable the evaluation bar, follow these steps:

1. **Login to PawnTown**: Go to pawn.town and log in to your account.
2. **Access Settings**: Click on your account at the top right corner, navigate to `Settings`, and then go to the `Stream` section.
3. **Toggle Evaluation Bar**: You can enable or disable the evaluation bar by toggling the switch.
