# ðŸ“‹ PGN Export

Every game you broadcast on Pawn Town is automatically saved in PGN format. This feature allows you and your viewers to download and analyze your games in your favorite chess software.

![Hello](/img/pgn-export.png)

## How to Export PGN

After your broadcast has finished and you've ended the stream, you can download the PGN file by following these steps:

1. **Open the recorded stream**: Go to your profile and click on the stream that
   includes the game you want to export.

2. **Download PGN**: All the games from the stream are listed on the right side of the screen. Click on the game you want to export, and the download will start automatically.
