## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A Pawn Town account
- The White Pawn app installed on your device

You can find links to create an account and download the app in the video description below.

## Step 1: Generate a Streaming Token

1. **Sign In**: Log into your Pawn Town account.
2. **Access Settings**: Click on your account at the top right corner, navigate to `Settings`, and then go to the `Stream` section.
3. **Generate Token**:
   - If you havenâ€™t already created a token, click on `Generate New Token`.
   - Once your token is generated, click on the `QR code` icon to get your QR code.

## Step 2: Use the White Pawn App to Scan the Token

1. **Open the App**: Launch the White Pawn app.
2. **Navigate**: On the home screen, tap the `PawnTown` card at the top right corner.
3. **Scan QR Code**:
   - Go to the sign-in screen and select `Scan Token QR Code`.
   - Use your device's camera to scan the QR code displayed on the PawnTown site.
4. **Confirmation**: Once scanned, you should see your streaming URL at the bottom. Ensure that it shows a green status and your username on the Home Screen.

## Step 3: Configure Streaming Options

1. **Enhancements**:
   - Enable `Auto Position Enhancements` for better visual quality.
   - Turn on `Live Evaluation` to display an evaluation bar during your stream.
2. **Notation Settings**: Set the move notation to your display preference.

## Step 4: Set Up Your Chessboard

1. **Choose a Compatible Chessboard**: Visit <a href="https://whitepawn.app">whitepawn.app</a> for a list of compatible chessboards.
2. **Turn On Chessboard**: Power on your compatible chessboard.
3. **Connect the Chessboard**:
   - In the White Pawn app, go to the `Chessboard` section.
   - If itâ€™s your first time, tap the small plus icon in the upper right corner, search your chessboard, and connect.

## Step 5: Start Streaming

1. **Open Stream Dialog**:
   - In WhitePawn on the home screen, click on the PawnTown Card, and choose `Start Sharing`.
   - Select `Open the Stream Board` to view the chessboard and clock that will be streamed.
2. **Customize Player Names**: Adjust the player names as necessary.

## Step 6: Go Live on Pawn Town

1. **Open PawnTown Stream**: Confirm you're streaming by opening your profile, and clicking on "Join Livestream"
2. **Play!**: Begin your game; your moves will be automatically updated and displayed on the Pawn Town website.
