# 🧑🏽‍💻 Developer Guide

At Pawntown, we're dedicated to ensuring a great experience for developers. Our platform, designed by **developers for developers** 👨‍💻👩‍💻, to provide frustration free integration of live chess streams on your website.

## 🔌 Embedding

We offer a simple and efficient way to integrate your chess stream on your website through iframes. You have the flexibility to embed either specific components or the entire PawnTown player, enabling your visitors to enjoy live chess streams directly from your site.
